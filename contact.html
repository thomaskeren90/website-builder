FAQ

Common Questions

What are the admission requirements?

You'll need your high school diploma (or equivalent), academic transcripts, a personal essay, and two letters of recommendation. International students may also need TOEFL/IELTS scores.

Are scholarships available?

Yes! We offer merit scholarships (up to 100% tuition), need-based financial aid, sports scholarships, and research assistantships for graduate students.

Can I visit the campus?

Absolutely. We offer guided campus tours every weekday at 10:00 and 14:00 WIB. You can also request a virtual tour or attend one of our Open House events.

Do you accept international students?

Yes, we welcome students from all over the world. We have students from 38 countries and offer visa support, language preparation, and dedicated international student services.
