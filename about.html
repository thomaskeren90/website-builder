Who We Are

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1985

Vanguard University was founded with a single vision: to make world-class education accessible to the brightest minds regardless of background. What started as a small college with 200 students has grown into one of the region's most respected institutions.

Today, we serve over 7,200 students from 38 countries, with 28 academic programs, 280 faculty members, and partnerships with universities and companies across the globe.

Our Journey

History & Milestones

1985

Founded as Vanguard College with 3 programs and 200 students in a single building.

1994

Earned national university status. Opened the Faculty of Engineering and Faculty of Business.

2003

First international partnership signed with Seoul National University. Exchange program launched.

2012

New 15-hectare campus opened. Student body reaches 5,000.

2019

Received international accreditation. Ranked in QS Asia Top 500.

2024

Launched Health Sciences faculty. 7,200+ students, 38 countries, 120+ industry partners.
